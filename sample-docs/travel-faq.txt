Title: Travel Insurance Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What is covered under travel insurance?
A: Medical emergencies, trip cancellation, lost baggage, and personal liability.
Q: How do I file a claim?
A: Contact our helpline within 24 hours and submit the claim form with supporting documents.
Q: Are pre-existing conditions covered?
A: No, pre-existing medical conditions are not covered.
